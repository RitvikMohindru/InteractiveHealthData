What have we done so far?

We finalized the structure for our webpage visualization, as shown above. We figured out which visualizations are most effective and expressive to show on our webpage. We also created a radar chart to show the relationship between the gamified vs. non-gamified surveys and physiological response over time. We are in the process of implementing the rest of our visualizations through D3.js, as well as the narrative-style, and-but-therefore structured webpage.

What will be the most challenging of your project to design and why?

The most challenging part in our project to design would be the and-but-therefore structure. This is because we are, at the moment, struggling to figure out how to integrate this into our narrative-style webpage. We understand what it means, and we have finalized the "and" and "therefore" parts; however, we have yet to determine the "but" part. This will be the most challenging part, as we need to find a way to introduce and solve the "obstacle" that adds complexity to our narrative.
